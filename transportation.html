Transportation

Taniti can be reached by air or by cruise ship. Once on the island, visitors can travel by bus, taxi, rental car, bike, or on foot in walkable areas.

Arriving to Taniti

Most visitors arrive to Taniti by air. The island is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Some visitors also arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week.

Ground Transportation

Visitors can get around Taniti by public bus, private bus, taxi, rental car, or bicycle. Public buses operate in Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day, while private buses serve the rest of the island. Taniti City is fairly flat and walkable, and many tourists also explore Merriton Landing on foot.
