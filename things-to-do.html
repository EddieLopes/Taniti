Things to Do

Explore beaches, rainforest, volcano, guided tours, and local activities.

Beaches

Relax on Taniti’s white sandy beaches and enjoy the beautiful coastline.

Rainforest

Explore lush rainforest trails, zip lining, and scenic island adventures.

Volcano

Explore Taniti’s scenic volcano area and enjoy one of the island’s most unique natural attractions.

Snorkeling & Fishing

Enjoy ocean adventures with snorkeling, charter fishing tours, and other water activities.

Museum & Art Galleries

Learn about local culture and history by visiting the museum and island art galleries.

Nightlife & Entertainment

Visit bars, a microbrewery, dance club, and local venues in the growing Merriton Landing area.
