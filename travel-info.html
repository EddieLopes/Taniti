Travel Information

Review important information to help you prepare for your visit to Taniti.

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks offer currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Power outlets

Power outlets on Taniti are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Medical Services

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Safety

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, although tourism has increased reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Local Laws & Holidays

Alcohol cannot be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18. Taniti also has many national holidays, and some attractions and restaurants may be closed on those days.
