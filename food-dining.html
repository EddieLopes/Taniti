Food & Dining

Taniti offers a variety of dining and grocery options for tourists during their stay.

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants. Five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Visitors can shop at two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
